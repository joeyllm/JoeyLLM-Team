# 🤗 Hugging Face: Models & Datasets

If GitHub is where our code lives, **Hugging Face is where our final products live.** This is the primary platform where the JoeyLLM team will publish our trained models and our highly refined datasets.

---

## 📦 What We Host Here

Hugging Face is the industry standard hub for machine learning. We will be using our organization page to host:

* **Datasets:** The polished, high-quality subsets and fully cleaned versions of the FineWeb data you are currently engineering.
* **Models:** The actual JoeyLLM weights, checkpoints, and fine-tuned iterations we produce in Term 2.



---

## 🤝 The Golden Rule: "Clean & Kind"

Hugging Face is a highly collaborative, public-facing open-source platform. Because our repositories here will be visible to the global AI community, **outside contributors, developers, and researchers will be directly interacting with our work.**

When you are writing documentation, creating Data/Model Cards, or responding to community discussions on Hugging Face, you must adhere to two core principles:

### 1. Be Clean (Professional & Clear)
* **Documentation is Everything:** A model or dataset without a proper "Card" is useless to the community. Ensure your Model Cards and Data Cards are meticulously detailed. Explain exactly what the data is, how your pipeline processed it, and what its limitations are.
* **No Messy Uploads:** Ensure files are correctly formatted (e.g., Parquet for datasets, Safetensors for models) before pushing them to the Hub.

### 2. Be Kind (Welcoming & Respectful)
* **Open-Source Etiquette:** When external developers open a Pull Request, ask a question in the Community tab, or point out a flaw in our dataset, treat them with absolute respect. 
* **Represent the Team:** You are representing the JoeyLLM project. Be helpful, constructive, and kind in all your public interactions. We want to build a community where people *want* to contribute to our work.

By keeping our Hugging Face presence clean and kind, we will attract top-tier contributors to help us push the project even further!
